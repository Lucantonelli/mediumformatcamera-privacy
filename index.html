Medium Format Camera — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Medium Format Camera ("the App") handles information when you use our application on your Android device.

1. Information We Collect

Medium Format Camera does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data of any kind. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an account, login, or registration.

2. Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's camera solely to allow you to take photographs within the App. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Photo Storage

Photos taken and processed within the App are saved directly to your device's gallery (local storage) upon your explicit confirmation. These photos remain exclusively on your device and are never uploaded, shared, or accessed by us.

4. Internet Access

Medium Format Camera does not use internet connectivity. No data is sent to or received from any server. All processing — including film simulation, color grading, and image saving — happens entirely on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No third party has access to any data through this App.

6. Children's Privacy

Medium Format Camera does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
